## ðŸ“š Part 5: Academic Research on Betting Edges

### Efficient Market Hypothesis in Sports Betting

**Key Studies:**

- Gray & Gray (1997) - NFL betting markets are largely efficient, but systematic biases exist
- Andrikogiannopoulou & Papakonstantinou (2011) - Behavioral biases (favorite-longshot bias) create inefficiencies
- Meier, Flepp & Franck (2021) - COVID-19 ghost games showed markets are semi-strong efficient

**Practical Takeaway:** Markets are efficient MOST of the time, but edges exist in:

- Early line releases (before sharp action)
- Breaking news events (injuries, weather)
- Behavioral biases (public overvalues favorites, primetime games)

### Line Movement as Predictor

**Key Finding:** Closing line is the most accurate predictor of game outcomes
**Implication:** CLV (beating the close) is the best measure of betting skill
**Strategy:** Bet early, capture value before lines sharpen

### Contrarian/Fading Public Strategies

**Research Evidence:**

- Public money overweights favorites, home teams, and primetime games
- Reverse line movement (line moves opposite public $) correlates with sharp action
- Simple "fade the public" yields ~52-53% win rate (marginal but real edge)

**Practical Application:** Flag games with >70% public on one side + line moving other way

### Key Numbers Analysis (NFL)

**Data Point:**

- 3 points: ~15% of NFL games decided by exactly 3
- 7 points: ~9% of NFL games decided by exactly 7
- Moving from -2.5 to -3 = ~3% swing in cover probability

**Strategy:** Pay extra to buy through key numbers, or fade games ON key numbers

### Rest and Schedule Advantages

**Research:**

- NFL teams off a bye: +1.5 to 2 point advantage (already baked into lines)
- NBA back-to-back road team: -2 to 3 point disadvantage
- Travel distance matters: West coast teams traveling East underperform

**Strategy:** Look for schedule spots not fully priced in (3rd game in 4 nights, etc.)

### Home Field Advantage Quantification

**Historical Averages:**

- NFL: ~2.5-3 points (declining over time, was 3+ pre-COVID)
- NBA: ~3-4 points
- MLB: ~0.5 runs (minimal)
- NHL: ~0.2-0.3 goals

**Trend:** Home field advantage has DECREASED significantly since 2020

### Weather Impact Studies

**Key Findings:**

- Wind 15+ mph: Under hits ~56% (significant)
- Rain: Minimal impact on NFL totals
- Cold (<32Â°F): Slight under lean
- Dome teams playing outdoors: Underperform by ~1 point

## ðŸŽ¯ Part 6: What Professional Handicappers Focus On

### Steam Moves and Syndicate Action

- **Definition:** Large coordinated bets across multiple books simultaneously
- **Detection:** Line moves 1+ point in <30 minutes at sharp books (Pinnacle, CIRCA)
- **Strategy:** Follow steam within 5-10 minutes for remaining value

### Opening vs Closing Line Analysis

- **Openers:** Released Sunday night (NFL) - most +EV opportunities
- **Closers:** Most accurate reflection of true probability
- **Edge Window:** First 12-24 hours after release

### Market Structure Understanding

- **Sharp Books:** Pinnacle, CIRCA, Bookmaker - set the market
- **Soft Books:** DraftKings, FanDuel - follow and add juice
- **Strategy:** Compare soft book lines to sharp book originals

### Correlation Plays (SGP/Parlays)

- **Example:** Team winning correlates with star player having big game
- **Edge:** Sportsbooks price SGPs assuming independence (wrong)
- **Strategy:** Find positive correlations priced as independent events

### Line Shopping Discipline

- **Rule:** Never bet without checking 5+ books minimum
- **Tool Need:** Real-time odds aggregation with alerts
- **ROI Impact:** +3-5% long-term from line shopping alone
